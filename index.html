The Truth Lies In The Words

Jack Freedman

Jack Freedman is the author of many psychology books. His books describe the interviews he has conducted with many people who have experienced traumatic events in their lives. Jack analyzes the behavior of his guests and describes their behavior, explaining their psychological nature.

The Truth Lies In The Words podcast is dedicated to discovering real human stories through individual storytelling. Host - Jack Freedman - invites to the studio guests who have experienced an amazing heavy drastic story in their lives. This time, Jack does not describe the story in the book, but introduces it live during an podcast interview.
